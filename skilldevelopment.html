Domestic Data Entry Operator

Data entry operator have good skills in typing and numerical entry and updating the data over the system. The data entry operator should know different techniques to do the task. The data entry course helps the student to acquire all the necessary technical skills required for the data entry through MS Office software.



Beauty Therapist

Beauty therapy courses can offer many advantages to the students. Skin care professionals often include salon-style laboratories where students blend skin care theory with actual skin care procedures. Such programs combine knowledge and study of the skin, clinical treatments and aesthetic applications with hands-on training.







Hair Stylist

There is a huge demand in the industry for a skilled and experienced Hair Stylist and so is the demand for Hair Stylist training courses. More and more people are becoming passionate about making hair styling career as it is turning out to be quite rewarding and satisfying.



Junior Software Developer

Junior Software Developer is the entry-level position in the field of software development. Their primary role is to learn the codebase, attend design meetings, write basic code, fix bugs. With the help of this course, students will acquire sufficient knowledge and skills that will enable him/her in writing and maintaining code on their own, working on bug fixes etc.







Training Partners







CMKKY (Chief Minister's Kaushalya Karnataka Yojane)

The government of Karnataka has set up the Skill Development, Entrepreneurship and Livelihood Department (SDEL) to help the youth to acquire necessary skills and expertise to increase their employability. Its aim is to undertake the functions of regulation, standardization, promotion, implementation and monitoring of all skill development initiatives in the state.



WCD-CMKKY (Women and Child Development- Chief Minister's Kaushalya Karnataka Yojane)

The government of Karnataka has set up the Skill Development, Entrepreneurship and Livelihood Department (SDEL) to help All women in Karnataka to become economically, socially and politically empowered, contributing as equal partners and acquire necessary skills and expertise to increase their employability



CDAY-NULM (Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana-National Urban Livelihoods Mission)

NULM will focus on organizing urban poor in their strong grassroots level institutions, creating opportunities for skill development leading to market-based employment and helping them to set up self-employment venture by ensuring easy access to credit. The Mission is aimed at providing shelter equipped with essential services to the urban homeless in a phased manner.
